Travel Poster

Dune

Definitely this last project was my favorite. When the class was given the project to choose a location for a destination poster, my mind went wild. At first I wanted to create a recently blown up Alderaan with the Death Star in the background. Have asteroids floating around, maybe a couple of Star Destroyers escorting the flagship. In the end my love for Dune won over.

While Star Wars has many iconic locations, in my mind Dune holds the best iconic sci-fi location with the planet of Arrakis. A desert planet filled with giant sand worms? Cool. But desert planet that people need to wear sweat suits to survive, a desert planet where the natives need to slide along the dunes with rhythm akin to the said giant worm, a desert planet where natives ride those giant worms? Badass. The only reason people even show up on this planet is for the spice melange, without it space travel would not be possible.

I would say my biggest problem with this project would be the path tools and "drawing" illustrations. I was never a good drawer and the path tools further made that a problem. I also went through multiple color pallets as I couldn't find a sand color that looked good enough.
