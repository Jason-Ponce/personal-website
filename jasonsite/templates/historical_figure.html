Historical Pamphlet

Emperor Justinian & Empress Theodora

The historical pamphlet was my second favorite project, mostly because the subject matter that I chose. I wanted to focus on not one but two imporant figures during the height of the Eastern Roman Empire. At first I thought about simply going with Emperor Justinian. Due to the accomplishments that he managed to bring. But none of it would be even have been possible for without his wife Theodora.

The biggest hurdle that I faced with this project was learning and planning in InDesign. Once I had laid down my pamphlets elements, to an untrained observer, the project would've looked "ok". But there was a clear lack of planning. After learning a bit more on InDesign, using the master pages, using paragraph styles. I came back to the elements and created a unified layout.

There are some issues that I still have with the pamphlet, but with time and more expierence I'll come back and improve on it more.
