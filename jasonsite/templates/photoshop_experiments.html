Smduge Effect

The smduge effect was the first experiment into Photoshop that I did. What was recommended was a bright photo so that when the smudge was applied, the smudge wouldn't just be a mess of grey goop. For the before pictures, I choose to use a group of flowers right outside Buley Library. Possibly with more colors, the end result would've been much more pleasing.

Underwater Effect

The underwater effect was a multistep process that required layers, text placement and manipulation, and color adjustment. One step that I was not able to follow through with is the bubbles that were added to the middleground. I would recommend to download the media provided in the lesson and following their steps before trying with another image.

Explosion

Looking at the explosion effect tutorial that adobe provides always looks better than what you achieve. Here in my picture I tried to apply the explosion effect on my dog. Next time, I would use another image as the source of the explosion to provide more color and variation to the mess that is the 3D explosion effect in this picture.
