Logo

The logo project was given to us to try and design a logo for one of three potential companies. There was a fourth option which I choose. The fourth option was to choose ourselves and design a logo for our needs. I wanted to make a logo that I could incorporate into my website. What I wanted was a logo that I could showcase to potential employees and use as a project.

My ideas came from.

-Using "Full Stack" imagery

-Making it look "techy"

-A cool logo


First Logo

The first logo that I designed came from influences from Digimon. I wanted to recreate a "digitizing" pattern that you would see in the show. Add in a bunch of boxes for a full stack look and this is what we get.

There were some other color variations that I messed around with. The first version had the boxes in the colors of the modern web developer. Like yellow based on the Javascript's logo.


Second Logo

My second logo was a continuation of the ideas that I had for my first logo. As I mentioned early, I used the colors of modern web developer packages. Like CSS, Javascript, SQL, ect. It's not perfect and its my least favorite. It's a bit too simple.


Third Logo

My third logo won in the end. You should've noticed by now. The idea behind this hits 2 out of the 3 points I wanted in my Logo It has a techy feel, it looks cool, and the third point which is a stretch, it has some full stack imagery. My initals are combined into a tag that one would write in HTML.

The image is was mostly inspired by the Monteserrat font I had found in GoogleFont, but I added to it to make my own design.
